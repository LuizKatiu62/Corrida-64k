In 2025, Luiz Silva was diagnosed with prostate cancer.
By God's grace, he fought and won.

Today — one year after surgery and celebrating 64 years of life — Luiz and his friends are running 64 km around the Little River Reservoir in Saint John, NB, as an act of gratitude for every kilometre of life.

Want to join? Walk, run, or just show up.
