The Challenge

What is the
64km Challenge?

On May 2, 2026, Luiz Silva turns 64 years old and celebrates in the best way he knows — running. The goal is to complete 64 kilometers in 3.3km loops at the Little River Reservoir Trail in Saint John, NB.

But this challenge is not just about distance. It is about gratitude for life, health, family and faith. It is about proving that the best chapter of life starts now.

My Story

From diagnosis
to the finish line

First and above all, I give all the glory to God — He was and continues to be ahead of every single step of this journey. His grace has been present in every moment, and I am deeply aware that none of this would be possible without Him.
"In early 2025, I was diagnosed with prostate cancer. The news was hard to receive, but my faith was greater than my fear. I went through surgery, and the results were remarkable — the procedure was a complete success.

What happened next surprised even my doctors. Thanks to a lifestyle built around physical activity — running, training and dedication — my recovery was extraordinary. In just 7 weeks I was back on the trail, running again.

From surgery day to early 2026, I had already run more than 800 kilometers. This 64km challenge on my 64th birthday is my way of saying thank you — to God, to life, to health, and to everyone who walked beside me through it all."
— Luiz Silva
